<h3><strong>CCA Framework</strong></h3>
<p>St Patrick’s School aims to provide a vibrant and robust co-curriculum
programme in the holistic development of our students. Our CCAs are platforms
for our students to develop talent, build character and develop leaders.
CCAs also help to facilitate the development of values and lifeskills in
each child. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Updated%20photos%20for%20CCA/cca%20overview.png">
</div>
<p>Here, we provide an environment for our students to pursue their interests
as far as possible. We believe that students tend to flourish in areas
there are interested in. Our robust and holistic CCA curriculum helps students
discover as well as develop their strengths and talents grounded in our
school’s values.</p>
<p>Our CCAs offer varied experiences to cater for our students’ interest
and their holistic development in their journey in St Patrick’s School.</p>
