<h4><strong>Profile</strong></h4>
<h5><strong>"Created to Create!"</strong></h5>
<p>The Media Club of St. Patrickâ€™s School is instrumental in providing print,
audio, video and photography support for school events and activities.</p>
<p>Trained by industry professionals, the 2 branches of the Media Club -
Photography and Audio-Videography work hand in hand to ensure the successful
running and coverage of events. Applying the skills and techniques taught
and adding their own creativity, our Patrician content creators produce
content to best represent our school values and culture, thereby fostering
a stronger school spirit in this digital age.</p>
